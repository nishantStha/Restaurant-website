WELCOME TO SHRESTHA'S KITCHEN

At Shrestha's Kitchen, we wish to offer our personal rendition of Newari gourmet cuisine with an innovative gastronomic take on traditional cooking methods, fine Newari produce and the legacy of our shared food heritage. Moreover, we regard it as a personal challenge to help bring about a revival of Newari cuisine and let its distinctive flavours and particular regional character brighten up the world.
